Our products

Supplying the very best.

We cooperate closely with our suppliers who are leading manufacturers, OEMs, stockists and principals from Europe, America, the Middle East and Asia to source and supply competitve and quaity products, inclusive of ex-stock products from project surplus, to cater for the requirements of our Clients’ development projects, construction and installation projects, commissioning contracts, and MRO related contracts.

We engage in the sourcing and supply of:

  • OCTG casing and tubing, 
  • Line pipes, piping, flanges, fittings, bolts & nuts, gaskets
  • Valves and actuators (subsea, offshore and onshore), 
  • Controls and instrumentation (subsea, offshore and onshore), 
  • Electrical products (cables, connectors, etc.),
  • Compressors and turbines, 
  • Pump packages, 
  • Subsea equipment, 
  • Static and process equipment, 
  • Electrical and mechanical spare parts, etc.
