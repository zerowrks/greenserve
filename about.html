About us

Dedicated and committed to quality and excellence.

Greenserve Oilfield Equipment Supplies & Services W.L.L. (Greenserve) is a Qatar based company specializing in procurement, sourcing, supply and trading of materials, capital equipment and spare parts for the Oil & Gas, Refining, Petrochemical, Power Generation and Water Treatment industries in the Middle East and Africa regions.

We engage in the sourcing and supply of OCTG casing and tubing, line pipes, piping, flanges, fittings, valves and actuators, controls and instrumentation, electrical products, compressors and turbines, pump packages, subsea equipment, static and process equipment, electrical and mechanical spare parts for field development projects, construction and installation projects, commissioning contracts, Maintenance, Repair and Operations (MRO) related contracts. Our Value Added Services include Project Delivery Services, Asset Performance Management Services and Technical Assistance Services.

Greenserve is an associate company of EKRON Enterprise WLL, an integrated solution provider of high quality services in the infrastructure industry in Qatar.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading value added supplier of materials, capital equipment, spare parts and provider of services for the oil & gas, Refining, Petrochemical, Power Generation and Water Treatment industries in Qatar and in the Middle East and Africa regions.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to dedicate and commit to quality and excellence in all supply solutions and services to Clients in Qatar and in the Middle East and Africa regions.

Our Core Values
Integrity, Quality, Trust and Customer Focus

Goals

  • To become a leading supply solution and services provider in the Oil & Gas, Refining, Petrochemical, Power Generation and Water Treatment industries in Qatar and in the Middle East and Africa regions
  • To provide competitive price, quality products and on-time services to customers whilst adhering to industry best practices and international standards
  • To provide services to the highest standard and quality
  • To ensure 100% customers’ satisfaction
