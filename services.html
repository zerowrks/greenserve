Our services

There when you need it.

Leveraging on our experience and our partners’ experience in the oil and gas industry, we offer a range of value added services to support our clients. We offer the following services:

Project Delivery Services

At Greenserve, our project management consultants and associates have several years of experience in the execution of energy projects. Leveraging on that experience, we apply an efficient Project and Technical Management framework to deliver improved performance in terms of Safety, Cost, Schedule, Constructability and Operability.

We also offer a full suite of Project / Owner’s Management Consulting Services for project execution phase.

Our services include:
- Project Strategy and Plan Development
- Contract Strategy Development
- Provision of Project Management Office services
- Technical Management
- Procurement Support
- Interface Management
- Project Risk Management
- Preparation of preliminary Budget and Schedule Estimates
- Information Management & Document Control
- Independent Review of Development Concepts and Technical Feasibility
- Independent Review of Preliminary Budget and Schedule Estimates
- Project Secondments

Asset Performance Management Services

At Greenserve, our consultants, in collaboration with our partner, provides through life asset performance management services in relation to owners’ oil & gas facilities, refinery and petrochemical plants, and power generation and water treatment industrial facilities:

Greenserve provides:
- Reliability Assessments
- Maintenance Strategy Development and Optimisation
- Effective Management of Critical Spares
- Integrity Management
- Corrosion Management
- Risk Based Inspection
- Operations Support

Technical Assistance Services
