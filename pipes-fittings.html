Stud Bolts, Nuts & Gaskets

We supply a wide range of alloy steel studs in Grades B7, B7M, L7, L7M, and B16, as well as stainless steel studs in Grades B8 and B8M, Grade 660, other materials and more, and a wide range of nuts to suit the selected stud bolts for their intended application.

We also supply a wide range of gaskets and seals: ring gaskets (R, RX, BX), spiral wound gaskets, metal jacketed gaskets, O-rings, graphite rings, etc.
