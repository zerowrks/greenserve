Our products

Valves & Actuators

Greenserve supplies a wide range of valve products and actuators for the oil & gas, refinery, petrochemical plants, industrial applications, subsea and cryogenic applications in accordance with ASME B16.34, API 6A/17D, 6D latest editions at the time of manufacture for various sizes and pressure classes. We offer a variety of corrosion resistant high pressure valves that are both reliable and repairable.

Valves

  • Ball valves (top entry, side entry, welded body, wedge type)
  • Gate valves (slab, expanding, bolted bonnet, pressure seal)
  • Double Block and Bleed valves
  • Globe Valves
  • Check valves (swing check, dual plate, nozzle check, tilting disk)
  • Flow Measurement and Control Valves (cage ball, choke, control globe, axial flow, orifice fittings
  • Butterfly valves
  • Compact valves
  • Subsea valves (API 6DSS, API 6A/17D, trunnion mounted ball valves, slab gate valves, double expanding gate valves, swing check valves, non-slam check valves, HIPPS, control valves)
  • Cryogenic valves (to -196 C; gate, globe, check, ball, available to 36”, to pressure class #2500)
  • HIPPS
  • Emergency Shutdown Valves
  • Instrument valves (needle valves, ball valves, plug valves, check valves, relief valves, and instrument manifolds)

Actuators

  • Electric actuators
  • Pneumatic and hydraulic actuators (HIPPS actuators, pneumatic actuators for gate, globe and axial flow valves, quarter turn actuators, etc.)
  • Process control actuators
  • Gearboxes and mounting kits
  • Subsea actuators (hydraulically operated and ROV driven gear boxes, diver or ROV retrieval systems)
  • Actuator accessories (subsea diver tools, compensation systems, extension systems, etc.)
